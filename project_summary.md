# AnanyaShop E-commerce SQL Project

## Project Objective

AnanyaShop is an e-commerce merchandise store for coders selling hoodies, mugs, stickers, notebooks, and accessories.

The goal of this project was to design a relational database and perform business analysis using SQL.

## Database Design

The database consists of 5 tables:

- Customers
- Products
- Orders
- Order Items
- Payments

Relationships were established using Primary Keys and Foreign Keys.

## Business Questions Answered

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. Which products generate the highest revenue?
3. Who are the top spending customers?
4. Which products are best sellers?
5. How many orders were cancelled?
6. Which cities generate the most revenue?
7. Which payment method is used most frequently?
8. What is the distribution of order statuses?

## Key Insights

- Revenue was concentrated among a few top-performing products.
- UPI emerged as a popular payment method.
- Certain cities contributed more revenue than others.
- A small percentage of orders were cancelled.
- Top customers generated a significant portion of total sales.
